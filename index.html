Privacy Policy

MigrainePal · Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MigrainePal ("the app", "we", "our") handles your information. We built MigrainePal around a simple principle: your health data belongs to you and stays on your device.

The short version

  • All attack logs, symptoms, triggers, medications, and notes you enter are stored locally on your iPhone, using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • We do not operate any server that receives, stores, or processes your health data. Nothing you log is transmitted off your device by us.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no trackers in this app.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone, because we never receive it in the first place.

What information the app collects

MigrainePal stores the information you choose to enter, including:

  • Attack records: start/end times, pain intensity, pain location, symptoms, and notes.
  • Triggers and relief methods you associate with an attack.
  • Medications you log, including dosage and your own effectiveness ratings.

All of the above is stored exclusively in the app's local database on your device. We have no access to it, and it is never sent to us or any third party.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, MigrainePal schedules local notifications directly on your device using Apple's notification framework. This does not involve sending your data anywhere — scheduling happens entirely on-device.

Doctor Report (PDF export)

When you generate a PDF summary of your attack history, that PDF is created locally on your device. Sharing it (for example, via Messages, Mail, or AirDrop) is entirely your choice and under your control through Apple's native share sheet — we are not involved in that transfer and do not receive a copy.

Subscriptions and payment

MigrainePal offers optional premium features via auto-renewable subscriptions and a one-time purchase, processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We do not collect or see your payment details — that information is handled by Apple according to Apple's own privacy practices. We only receive confirmation of your entitlement status (i.e., whether you have an active purchase), not any payment or billing information.

Data deletion

Because everything is stored locally, you are always in control of your data:

  • You can delete individual attack records at any time within the app.
  • Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

Since we never receive or store your data on any server, there is no separate "delete my account" request to make with us — deleting the app accomplishes this directly.

Children's privacy

MigrainePal is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 17.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Medical disclaimer: MigrainePal is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a personal tracking tool intended to help you organize information for discussions with your healthcare provider. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your symptoms and treatment.
